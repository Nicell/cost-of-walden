About

This tool offers a simple way to see how much it would've roughly cost to live at Walden pond based on Thoreau's list of materials he had in his chapter Economy. You can also play around with quantities, if say, you wanted to have four windows instead of two! See how much your cabin on Walden pond would cost.

The prices adjusted for inflation are calculated using the CPIs calculated by Robert Sahr of Oregon State University, which can be found here.
